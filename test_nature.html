Structural basis of PROTAC-mediated BRD4 degradation via CRBN ternary complex formation

Grace E. Bemis, Mark F. Calabrese, Jing Wang & Ian E. Gentle
Received 12 April 2023 · Accepted 22 August 2023 · Published 04 September 2023
Download PDF Download ePub

Abstract

PROTAC-mediated targeted protein degradation has emerged as a promising therapeutic modality, yet the structural determinants governing ternary complex formation between a PROTAC, its target, and an E3 ligase remain incompletely understood. Here we report cryo-EM structures of dBET6-mediated BRD4–CRBN–DDB1 ternary complexes at 3.2 Å resolution, revealing cooperative contacts absent in binary complexes.

Methods

Full crystallographic and cryo-EM methods are described in Supplementary Methods. Coordinates and structure factors have been deposited in the PDB (accession codes 8T4A and 8T4B). See Supplementary Information for detailed protocols.
